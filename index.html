Welcome to Barton Commercial

We are a commercial car leasing specialist, we have supplied lease vehicles to many satisfied businesses in the UK.


We work closely with manufacturers, large motor groups and key finance partners to offer some of the most competitive leasing rates in the market.

For more information about our offering, please get in contact with our team and we will do our upmost to deliver you the service that you require.
  • Fixed Monthly payment – no extra payments
  • Low capital expenditure – low rentals and deposits
  • No depreciation risks for our customers
  • No need to sell the vehicle – just send back the keys
  • VAT recoverable for business use
  • Full warranty – all vehicles come with a full manufacturers’ warranty which cover the full period.

Car Leasing

We offer leased vehicles on 12, 24 and, 36 month terms. At the end of the contract you simply hand the car back.

Lease Purchase

Business lease purchase is an alternative finance option for those business’ that with to purchase the vehicle at the end of the contract. A Business lease purchase is a series of low monthly rentals over the term followed by a balloon payment. This balloon payment is in order to buy the vehicle at the end of the agreement and will be based on how much the vehicle is worth.
